Hollow Knight Charms Guide

**Utility Charms:**
*   **Wayward Compass**
    *   **Description:** Whispers its location to the bearer whenever a map is open, allowing wanderers to pinpoint their current location.
    *   **Cost:** 220 Geo (Purchased from Iselda in Dirtmouth).
*   **Gathering Swarm**
    *   **Description:** A swarm of tiny, harmless creatures that follow the bearer and collect any loose Geo.
    *   **Cost:** 300 Geo (Purchased from Sly in Dirtmouth).
*   **Stalwart Shell**
    *   **Description:** Builds resilience. When recovering from damage, the bearer will remain invulnerable for longer.
    *   **Cost:** 200 Geo (Purchased from Sly in Dirtmouth).
*   **Soul Catcher**
    *   **Description:** Used by shamans to draw more SOUL from the world around them. Increases the amount of SOUL gained when striking an enemy with the nail.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Ancestral Mound (West of Elder Baldur).
*   **Soul Eater**
    *   **Description:** Forgotten shaman artifact, used to draw SOUL from still-living creatures. Greatly increases the amount of SOUL gained when striking an enemy with the nail.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Resting Grounds (Requires Desolate Dive).
*   **Dashmaster**
    *   **Description:** Bears the likeness of a nimble bug known as the Dashmaster. The bearer can dash more often as well as dash downwards.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Fungal Wastes (South of Mantis Village).
*   **Sprintmaster**
    *   **Description:** Bears the likeness of a strange bug known only as 'The Sprintmaster'. Increases the running speed of the bearer.
    *   **Cost:** 400 Geo (Purchased from Sly after obtaining the Shopkeeper's Key).
*   **Grubsong**
    *   **Description:** Contains the gratitude of freed grubs. Gain SOUL when taking damage.
    *   **Cost:** Reward from Grubfather for freeing 10 Grubs.
*   **Grubberfly's Elegy**
    *   **Description:** Contains the gratitude of grubs who will move to the next stage of their lives. Imbues weapons with a holy strength. When the bearer is at full health, they will fire beams of white hot energy from their nail.
    *   **Cost:** Reward from Grubfather for freeing all 46 Grubs.
*   **Fragile Heart**
    *   **Description:** Increases the health of the bearer, allowing them to take more damage. Breaks upon death.
    *   **Cost:** 350 Geo (Purchased from Leg Eater in Fungal Wastes).
*   **Unbreakable Heart**
    *   **Description:** Increases the health of the bearer, allowing them to take more damage.
    *   **Cost:** 12000 Geo (Upgraded from Fragile Heart by Divine).
*   **Fragile Greed**
    *   **Description:** Causes enemies to drop more Geo when they are defeated. Breaks upon death.
    *   **Cost:** 250 Geo (Purchased from Leg Eater in Fungal Wastes).
*   **Unbreakable Greed**
    *   **Description:** Causes enemies to drop more Geo when they are defeated.
    *   **Cost:** 9000 Geo (Upgraded from Fragile Greed by Divine).
*   **Fragile Strength**
    *   **Description:** Increases the damage of the bearer's nail. Breaks upon death.
    *   **Cost:** 600 Geo (Purchased from Leg Eater in Fungal Wastes).
*   **Unbreakable Strength**
    *   **Description:** Increases the damage of the bearer's nail.
    *   **Cost:** 15000 Geo (Upgraded from Fragile Strength by Divine).

**Combat Charms:**
*   **Heavy Blow**
    *   **Description:** Increases the force of the bearer's nail, causing enemies to recoil further when hit.
    *   **Cost:** 350 Geo (Purchased from Sly in Dirtmouth).
*   **Quick Slash**
    *   **Description:** Allows the bearer to swing their nail considerably faster.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Kingdom's Edge.
*   **Longnail**
    *   **Description:** Increases the Nail's range.
    *   **Cost:** 300 Geo (Purchased from Salubra in Forgotten Crossroads).
*   **Mark of Pride**
    *   **Description:** Greatly increases the range of the bearer's nail.
    *   **Cost:** Reward from defeating the Mantis Lords in Fungal Wastes.
*   **Fury of the Fallen**
    *   **Description:** When close to death, the bearer's nail gains a terrible power.
    *   **Cost:** Found in King's Pass.
*   **Thorns of Agony**
    *   **Description:** When the bearer takes damage, thorns burst out, dealing damage to nearby foes.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Greenpath.
*   **Baldur Shell**
    *   **Description:** Protects the bearer with a hard shell while focusing SOUL. The shell is not invincible and will break after absorbing too much damage.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Greenpath.
*   **Flukenest**
    *   **Description:** Living charm born in the gut of a Flukemarm. Transforms the Vengeful Spirit spell into a horde of volatile baby flukes.
    *   **Cost:** Reward from defeating the Flukemarm in Royal Waterways.
*   **Defender's Crest**
    *   **Description:** Unique charm bestowed by the King of Hallownest to his most loyal knight. Scratched and dirty, but still cared for. Causes the bearer to emit a heroic odour. (Emits a toxic cloud that damages enemies).
    *   **Cost:** Reward from defeating the Dung Defender in Royal Waterways.
*   **Glowing Womb**
    *   **Description:** Drains the SOUL of its bearer and uses it to birth hatchlings. The hatchlings have no desire to eat or live, and will sacrifice themselves to protect their parent.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Forgotten Crossroads (Search the body of a large Aspid Mother).
*   **Shaman Stone**
    *   **Description:** Said to contain the knowledge of past generations of shaman. Increases the power of spells.
    *   **Cost:** 220 Geo (Purchased from Salubra in Forgotten Crossroads).
*   **Spell Twister**
    *   **Description:** Naturally formed within a crystal over a long period. Draws in SOUL from the surrounding air. Reduces the SOUL cost of spells.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Soul Sanctum.
*   **Steady Body**
    *   **Description:** Keeps the bearer from recoiling backwards when they strike an enemy with a nail.
    *   **Cost:** 120 Geo (Purchased from Salubra in Forgotten Crossroads).

**Healing & SOUL Charms:**
*   **Quick Focus**
    *   **Description:** A charm containing a crystal lens. Increases the speed of focusing SOUL, allowing the bearer to heal damage faster.
    *   **Cost:** 800 Geo (Purchased from Salubra in Forgotten Crossroads).
*   **Deep Focus**
    *   **Description:** A charm containing a larger crystal lens. Focus SOUL at a slower rate, but recover 2 masks of damage.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Crystal Peak.
*   **Lifeblood Heart**
    *   **Description:** Contains a living core that bleeds precious lifeblood. When resting, the bearer will gain a coating of lifeblood that protects from a modest amount of damage.
    *   **Cost:** 250 Geo (Purchased from Salubra in Forgotten Crossroads).
*   **Lifeblood Core**
    *   **Description:** Contains a living core that bleeds precious lifeblood. When resting, the bearer will gain a coating of lifeblood that protects from a large amount of damage.
    *   **Cost:** Found in The Abyss (Requires King's Brand).
*   **Joni's Blessing**
    *   **Description:** Blessed by Joni, the kindly heretic. Transfigures vital fluids into blue lifeblood. The bearer will have a healthier shell and can take more damage, but they will not be able to heal themselves by focusing SOUL.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Howling Cliffs.
*   **Hiveblood**
    *   **Description:** Golden nugget of the Hive's precious hardened nectar. Heals the bearer's wounds over time, allowing them to regularly gain health without focusing SOUL.
    *   **Cost:** Reward from defeating the Hive Knight in The Hive.

**Movement & Exploration Charms:**
*   **Spore Shroom**
    *   **Description:** When focusing SOUL, the bearer will emit a cloud of damaging spores.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Fungal Wastes (Requires Mantis Claw, near a pool of acid).
*   **Sharp Shadow**
    *   **Description:** Allows the bearer's shadow dash to deal damage to enemies it passes through.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Deepnest.
*   **Shape of Unn**
    *   **Description:** While focusing SOUL to heal, transforms the Knight into a small slug form for slow movement and the evasion of some attacks.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Greenpath (Requires Isma's Tear, located beneath the Lake of Unn region).
*   **Nailmaster's Glory**
    *   **Description:** Increases the speed at which the Nail Arts can be charged.
    *   **Cost:** Reward from Sly after learning all 3 Nail Arts.

**Dream & Void Charms:**
*   **Dream Wielder**
    *   **Description:** Gives the bearer the ability to use the Dream Nail more rapidly and gather more SOUL when striking foes.
    *   **Cost:** Reward for collecting 500 Essence for the Seer.
*   **Dreamshield**
    *   **Description:** Conjures a shield that slowly rotates around the Knight's position, blocks certain projectiles, and deals Nail damage when colliding with an enemy.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Resting Grounds.

**Grimm Troupe Charms:**
*   **Grimmchild**
    *   **Description:** A young, but fierce, creature that aids the bearer in combat. Grows stronger by consuming Flames.
    *   **Cost:** Obtained from Troupe Master Grimm in Dirtmouth at the start of The Grimm Troupe quest.
*   **Carefree Melody**
    *   **Description:** A charm created for those who would forsake the Grimm Troupe. Bestows good luck upon the bearer. Has a chance to negate incoming damage.
    *   **Cost:** Obtained by banishing the Grimm Troupe.

**Hidden Dreams Charms:**
*   **Weaversong**
    *   **Description:** Summons three little weavers that will attack foes with their tiny, sharp claws.
    *   **Cost:** Found in Deepnest.

**Kingsoul & Void Heart:**
*   **Kingsoul**
    *   **Description:** Holy charm symbolising a union between higher beings. The bearer will slowly generate SOUL.
    *   **Cost:** Combined from two halves (White Fragment from White Lady, White Fragment from The Pale King's Corpse).
*   **Void Heart**
    *   **Description:** An emptiness that was, is, and ever will be. Unifies the void under the bearer's will. Removes the Kingsoul's notch cost and silences the screams of the Siblings.
    *   **Cost:** Obtained by traversing The Abyss with Kingsoul equipped.
